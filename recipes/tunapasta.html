TunaPasta

This dish is quick and easy to make, requiring little preparation and tasting great, with plenty of protein and carbs to keep you going.

Within 15 minutes this recipe can easily be created. Different sauces can be used in combination with mayo to create more depth to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tuna
  • Sweetcorn
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil a saucepan of water, roughly just over halfway.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add 250g dried pasta and 1 tsp of salt
  3. Place 250g canned tuna, 100g sweetcorn and 75g mayonnaise into a bowl.
  4. After 10 minutes, remove the pasta from the heat and immediately strain it.
  5. Once strained well, mix the pasta with the tuna, sweetcorn and mayo and add black pepper.
  6. DONE!
  7. For extra flavour, you can mix ketchup or sriracha in with the dish at the end too.
